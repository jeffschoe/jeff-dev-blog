---
title: "Older Sisters"
date: "2026-03-25"
---

Having an older sister is like living with a self-appointed dictator who also happens to give surprisingly good advice… sometimes. She’ll steal your stuff, roast you mercilessly, and act like the family CEO—but hey, she’s also the only one who’ll fight your battles… if it benefits her. Love her, tolerate her, but never, ever underestimate her sass.
